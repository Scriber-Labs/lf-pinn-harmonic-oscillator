1. Show how wedge products act on two tangent vectors $v,w \in T_(q_0,p_0)(\mathcal{M})$ (i.e., two vectors in a tangent space corresponding to a point in phase-space $\mathcal{M}$) where $v=(\dot{q}_v, \dot{p}_v)=(2,-3)$ and $w=(\dot{q}_w, \dot{p}_w)=(1,4)$. 
    
    **Solution:** Apply 
        $$(\partial_q \wedge \partial_p)(v,w) = \partial_q(v)\partial_p(w) - \partial_q(w)\partial_p(w)$$
    to $v=(\dot{q}_v,\dot{p}_v)=(2,-3)$ and $w=(\dot{q}_w, \dot{q}_w)=(1,4)$.

    - Note: $a=(\dot{q}_a, \dot{p}_a)$ can be written $$a=\dot{q}_a(\partial_q) + \dot{q}_a(\partial_p)$$ where $\partial_p = \frac{\partial}{\partial p}$ and $\partial_q = \frac{\partial}{\partial q}$.
